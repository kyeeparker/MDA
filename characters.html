Creators of Ma Douce Amertume

Ma Douce Amertume has been created by three main people, Subcontious, Kye Parker and Louie Finchi.

Subcontious

The lead developer and the main programmer of the game, Subcontious started with the concept of Ma Douce Amertume and started to bring his ideas to life.

Kye Parker

The lead animator and the secondary programmer of the game, Kye Parker met Subcontious in the start of Ma Douce Amertume, both became best friends and Kye started working on the project along with Subcontious and Louie Finchi.

Louie Finchi

The lead modeller of the game, Louie Finchi is Subcontious's childhood friend, since Subcontious started working on Ma Douce Amertume, Louie started helping him bringing every single model that used to be on paper, to life.
